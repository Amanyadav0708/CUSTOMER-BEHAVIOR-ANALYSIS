# 📊 Customer Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
Customer Behavior Analysis is an end-to-end Data Analytics project that analyzes customer shopping patterns to uncover purchasing trends, customer preferences, and business opportunities.

This project combines **Python**, **MySQL**, **SQL**, and **Power BI** to transform raw customer shopping data into meaningful business insights through an interactive dashboard.

## 🎯 Business Problem
Retail businesses collect large amounts of customer purchase data every day. This project helps answer:
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which age groups contribute the most sales?
- What is the average purchase amount?
- How many customers subscribe to membership programs?
- Which shipping methods are most preferred?

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### Subscription Status
- 70.77% customers are **not subscribed**
- 29.23% customers are **subscribed**

### Revenue by Category
- Clothing generates the highest revenue.
- Accessories rank second.
- Footwear and Outerwear contribute less revenue.

### Sales by Category
- Clothing has the highest sales volume.
- Outerwear has the lowest sales.

### Revenue by Age Group
- Young Adults generate the highest revenue.
- Seniors contribute the least.

### Sales by Age Group
- Young Adults purchase most frequently.
- Senior customers have the lowest purchase frequency.
